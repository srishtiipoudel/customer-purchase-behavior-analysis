# Customer Purchase Behavior Analysis
An end-to-end analytics project focused on understanding customer purchasing patterns, spending behavior, and subscription trends using Python, PostgreSQL, and Power BI.
The analysis is based on 3,900 customer transactions and covers data cleaning, exploratory analysis, SQL-based business reporting, and dashboard development to generate actionable business insights.

## Project Objectives
* Analyze customer spending patterns and purchasing behavior
* Identify high-value customer segments
* Evaluate the impact of discounts and subscriptions on revenue
* Understand product performance across categories
* Present findings through an interactive dashboard

## Tools
* Python (Pandas, NumPy)
* PostgreSQL
* Power BI
* Jupyter Notebook

## Analysis Highlights
* Revenue analysis by customer demographics
* Customer segmentation based on purchase history
* Subscriber vs. non-subscriber spending comparison
* Product performance and customer ratings analysis
* Discount and promotion effectiveness
* Revenue contribution by age group
